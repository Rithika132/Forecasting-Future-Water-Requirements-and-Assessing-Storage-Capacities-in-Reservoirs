About Project

Forecasting Future Water Requirements and Assessing Storage Capacities in Reservoirs is a smart water management project. The main objective of this project is to predict future water demand by analyzing population growth, rainfall, and historical water usage.

This project helps government authorities and water management departments estimate future water needs and determine whether existing reservoirs have sufficient storage capacity. It also supports better planning during droughts, improves water conservation, and ensures efficient distribution of water resources.

The system uses forecasting techniques to estimate future water requirements and provides useful information for sustainable water resource management. It helps reduce water shortages and supports long-term environmental planning.
