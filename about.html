About Our Project

🌊 Project Overview

Forecasting Future Water Requirements and Assessing Storage Capacities in Reservoirs is a smart water management project. It predicts future water demand using population growth, rainfall, and historical water usage data. The project also checks whether the available reservoirs have enough storage capacity to meet future needs.

🎯 Objectives

  • Predict future water demand accurately.
  • Analyze reservoir storage capacity.
  • Support drought management.
  • Reduce water shortages.
  • Promote sustainable water usage.

⭐ Key Features

  • Water demand forecasting
  • Reservoir capacity analysis
  • Population growth calculation
  • Historical data analysis
  • Simple and user-friendly interface

⚙ Technologies Used

  • HTML5
  • CSS3
  • JavaScript
  • Node.js
  • JSON Database

📈 Benefits

  • Supports government water planning.
  • Improves reservoir management.
  • Helps save water resources.
  • Provides accurate future predictions.
